Internship Portfolio Summary

This repository documents the practical work completed during the 15-day Verilog HDL Internship Program conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at TKM College of Engineering (TKMCE). It contains a systematic collection of digital hardware design projects, simulation testbenches, waveform analyses, and implementation results developed using Xilinx Vivado Design Suite. The portfolio reflects hands-on experience in designing, modeling, and verifying both combinational and sequential digital circuits through Verilog HDL.
